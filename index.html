About

My name is Cayla, and I'm a freshman at Dripping Springs High School. I have always loved baking so when I got to the age most girls start babysitting to make some money I made cupcakes! I thought it was tons of fun to make new flavors of cupcakes and find the yummiest one.

At first my parents provided me with all the ingredients I needed. When we moved here in June of 2014 I really got Cayla's Cupcakes going. I started paying for my own ingredients. It felt very good to be an entrepreneur and I cannot wait for summer to roll around so I can take orders any day!
